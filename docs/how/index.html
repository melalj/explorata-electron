How to get your data from Facebook

  1. Go to facebook.com/dyi
  2. Click on “Download Your Information”
  3. Under “Request Copy”, choose ‘JSON’ for ‘Format’ and ‘Low’ for ‘Media Quality’, like so:
  4. Scroll to “Your information” and click on “Deselect All”.
  5. Scroll to ‘Messages’ and click on the checkbox.
  6. Scroll up to the top and click on ‘Create File’.
  7. Well done! You should see this banner under ‘Request Copy’.

Now, we just have to wait until Facebook creates your file (up to 6 hours). Facebook will send you both a notification and an email when the file is ready.

Finally, download your file and then unzip it. You just have to drag the ‘messages’ folder onto Explorata!
