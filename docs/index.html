Explorata

Explore your Facebook Messenger data

Explorata is an open-source tool that allows you to see your messages in a fun, meaningful, and perhaps nostalgic way.

Private by design, your data is processed on your machine, never uploaded — you can even use Explorata offline.

Download Explorata v1.0.0 Fork on GitHub
How to get your data from Facebook

Features

Visualise all the messages you ever sent. All-time and yearly view.

Click on any date to see all conversations from that day.

Click on any conversation to read its contents.

See with whom you exchange most messages. All-time and yearly view.

Click on any person's name to see cool stats and be able to read the first 10,000 messages.

Including how many GIFs and emojis you exchanged!
